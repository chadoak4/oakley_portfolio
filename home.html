Hi there, I'm Chad!

I know you are excited about my work, but first discover the passion, creativity, and optimism, that runs wild inside of me.

It was in the business operations of the healthcare industry where my initial career started. Here, I became a passionate and creative leader with a proven track record of innovative problem solving and interpersonal effectiveness. I accepted any and every challenge thrown my way, and not only did I overcome each adversity, I shattered all expectations. Unfortunately, this led to extreme boredom, and I knew change was needed.

I then turned to my passion for technology, a field that is ever expanding, and knew the difficulties of the challenges that awaited. I developed a hunger to learn as much as I could and online courses weren't cutting it. I wanted to truly test my will and entered in the technology field by joining The Iron Yard

The Iron Yard is a coding school, that offers an intensive 12-week immersive program in a variety of technical programming languages. The Iron Yard is not an easy path. I spent countless hours jamming as much knowledge as possible into my brain. 80+ hours a week of learning to code was the biggest challenge I have faced to date. I just wasn't flirting with my comfort zone, I was pushed way beyond it. After 12-weeks of high emotions, little sleep, and continually being pushed to the brink, I graduated as a certified full-stack developer, that specializes in ruby on rails. This leads me to the present where I am looking to join you and or your team to accomplish the unthinkable.

When I am not coding my face off, I am competing. Whether it's in futbol, ping-pong, tic-tac-toe, you name it, I play to win. If i'm not kicking your butt at something, its probably because I am off reading. A favorite quote of mine from Dr Who describes the power of knowledge perfectly: You want weapons?
